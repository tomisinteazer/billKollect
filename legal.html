Frequently Asked Questions

What is BillKollect?

BillKollect is a digital commerce and payments platform that allows users to make payments, transfer funds, and access various services like airtime recharge, utility bill payments, and more, all from one convenient platform.

How do I create a BillKollect account?

To create a BillKollect account, visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. You'll need to provide your name, email address, phone number, and create a secure password. You may also need to verify your identity by providing additional information.

Is BillKollect available in my country?

BillKollect is currently available in select countries. Please check our website or contact our customer support to confirm availability in your region.
